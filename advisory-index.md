## Advisory Lifecycle Model

Each advisory in this repository follows a structured lifecycle:

Research → Validation → Responsible Disclosure → Vendor Confirmation → Patch Development → CVE Assignment → Public Disclosure

This model follows coordinated vulnerability disclosure practices.

---

## Disclosure Policy

To support responsible disclosure:

- Technical exploit details are restricted until vendor fixes are released
- Advisory summaries may remain visible without sensitive information
- CVE identifiers are added after publication
- Technical analysis may be expanded post-disclosure
